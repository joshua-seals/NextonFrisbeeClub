Ultimate Frisbee aka Ultimate

Ultimate Frisbee is a non-contact, self-officiated game which blends athletic skills of many sports like basketball, football and soccer. It is a fast paced game between two teams where the objective is to score by catching the disc in the opponent's end zone. Key rules include: no running with the disc, a 10-second stall count for the thrower, and a strong emphasis on sportsmanship and fair play, known as the 'Spirit of the Game'.
