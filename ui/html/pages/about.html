Origin Story

Nexton Frisbee Club was started during 2020 by Benjamin Bethea. Fighting the COVID blues, he thought an ultimate frisbee group would be a great way to make some new friends in his new neighborhood and get some much needed excercise while staying reasonably safe. Others too shared the desire and soon it became a weekly event!
